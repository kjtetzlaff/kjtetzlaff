Welcome!

Minnetonka Mugs is a company that creates custom mugs and drinkware. They focus on local Minnesota-themed designs, but also offer fully customizable options for businesses, events, and personal gifts. Their products are high-quality, durable, and dishwasher-safe. They emphasize their commitment to supporting local artists and using eco-friendly materials.

Before you get too excited to order, we want to be upfront: Minnetonka Mugs is not a real company. This website is a demonstration project created for Web Design 8 at MME and MMW. All the images you see are AI-generated, showcasing what's possible with modern web design and content creation. We hope you enjoy exploring this example!
